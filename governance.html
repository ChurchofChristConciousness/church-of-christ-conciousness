Order & Stewardship

Authority Aligned with Divine Law

Covenant Governance

Governance within the Church of Christ Consciousness exists not for control, but for alignment — ensuring that all stewardship, trust, and authority operate in harmony with divine law and righteous order.

Stewardship & Trust

All offices, assets, and responsibilities are held in trust before God. Authority is exercised as stewardship, accountability, and service — not ownership. Order preserves liberty; covenant preserves truth.
