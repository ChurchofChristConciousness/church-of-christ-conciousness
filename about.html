Authority & Lineage

The Church of Christ Consciousness stands upon spiritual authority derived not from institutions of man, but from divine lineage — the inheritance of Sonship restored through Christ.

The Living Church

This Church is not constructed of stone alone, but of living souls ordered in righteousness, wisdom, and covenant. Christ is the cornerstone; all else aligns by Him.
