The Christ Consciousness Church stands within a documented lineage of religious assembly, stewardship, and public service rooted in historic land use and ecclesiastical continuity.

The property known as 77 Brinkerhoff Street has long served as a place of gathering, instruction, reflection, and charitable activity consistent with religious and public benefit purposes.

19th Century

The land and surrounding structures are recorded as sites of residence, assembly, and civic participation, reflecting early American religious and communal life.

20th Century

Continued use for learning, fellowship, and community-centered purposes, maintaining alignment with religious observance and charitable function.

21st Century

The site is formally stewarded under ecclesiastical trust principles, preserving its sacred character, lawful protection, and continuity of use.
