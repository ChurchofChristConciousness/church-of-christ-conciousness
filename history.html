    Founding & Lineage

    The Church of Christ Consciousness is grounded in a lineage of spiritual assembly, stewardship, and public service, rooted in historic land use and lawful trust governance.

    Property Stewardship

    The property at 77 Brinkerhoff Street has long served as a site for gathering, instruction, reflection, and community engagement, aligned with religious and charitable purposes.

    Timeline of Historical Stewardship

    19th Century: Documented as a place of residence, assembly, and civic participation, reflecting early religious and communal life.

    20th Century: Continued use for learning, fellowship, and community-centered purposes, maintaining alignment with public benefit.

    21st Century: Formally stewarded under ecclesiastical trust principles, preserving its religious character and lawful protection.
