The Christ Consciousness Church is held and administered under ecclesiastical trust principles, ensuring lawful stewardship, continuity of purpose, and protection from commercial or political interference.

Governance is exercised solely for religious, charitable, and educational purposes, in accordance with divine law, natural law, and the public good.

Ecclesiastical Trust: All property and assets are held in trust for sacred use, immune from private ownership claims and commercial exploitation.

Stewardship: Trustees serve as caretakers, not owners, bound by fiduciary responsibility and spiritual accountability.

Continuity: The trust structure ensures uninterrupted religious function across generations.

Public Benefit: Use of trust assets must serve the congregation and the broader community without profit motive.
