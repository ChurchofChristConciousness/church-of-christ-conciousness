Projects and ministries of The Christ Consciousness Church are undertaken as acts of stewardship, service, and public benefit, aligned with spiritual, educational, and charitable purposes.

All initiatives are non-commercial, non-political, and administered in accordance with ecclesiastical trust principles.

  • Land Stewardship: Preservation, maintenance, and reverent use of church-held land for sanctuary and community benefit.
  • Educational Instruction: Teaching, study groups, and contemplative learning centered on Christ Consciousness.
  • Public Service: Outreach, aid, and cooperative service initiatives consistent with charitable purpose.
  • Historical Preservation: Protection and documentation of sacred sites and trust-held property.
