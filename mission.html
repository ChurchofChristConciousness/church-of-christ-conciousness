Our Mission

The mission of The Christ Consciousness Church is to uphold divine law through worship, instruction, and faithful stewardship of trust property, offering a sanctuary for spiritual growth, moral order, and public benefit.

Foundational Commitments

Worship

The regular observance of prayer, contemplation, and sacramental life in reverence to divine authority.

Instruction

Teaching scripture, moral philosophy, and spiritual discipline to foster awareness, responsibility, and lawful living.

Stewardship

Careful management of land, resources, and trust assets solely for ecclesiastical and public benefit.

Sanctuary

Maintaining spaces of peace, refuge, and order for spiritual reflection and community well-being.
