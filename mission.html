Our Mission

Anchored in Christ, Flowing in Consciousness

Purpose & Vision

The Church of Christ Consciousness seeks to awaken divine awareness, illuminate spiritual truths, and cultivate Christ-centered community. Our mission flows like the ocean — constant, renewing, and far-reaching.

Living the Mission

Through teaching, stewardship, public service, and sacred gatherings, we bring the light of Christ consciousness into all aspects of life. The Church acts as a beacon, guiding members toward higher awareness and communal harmony.

“He leads me beside still waters. He restores my soul.” — Psalm 23:2-3
