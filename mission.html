The mission of The Christ Consciousness Church is to uphold divine law through worship, instruction, and faithful stewardship, providing sanctuary, order, and public benefit.

The Church exists as a living ecclesiastical body, dedicated to the cultivation of Christ Consciousness — a lived awareness of truth, compassion, and spiritual responsibility.

Through lawful trust governance, non-commercial practice, and service-oriented ministry, the Church safeguards sacred space, protects religious liberty, and offers instruction for spiritual and moral development.

All activities are conducted without private inurement and solely for religious, educational, and charitable purposes consistent with divine and natural law.
