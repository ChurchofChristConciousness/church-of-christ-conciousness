The mission of The Christ Consciousness Church is to uphold divine law through worship, instruction, and faithful stewardship, cultivating a living awareness of Christ Consciousness expressed through truth, compassion, and service.

The Church exists as a lawful ecclesiastical body, holding land, resources, and authority in trust for spiritual use and public benefit, without private inurement or commercial purpose.

“Where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is liberty.”
— 2 Corinthians 3:17

Through prayer, teaching, and service, the Church seeks to provide sanctuary, order, and continuity for present and future generations, maintaining fidelity to sacred responsibility and lawful governance.
