Awakening the Living Presence of Christ Within

Our Sacred Mission

The Church of Christ Consciousness exists to restore the living awareness of Christ within humanity — not as distant doctrine, but as present truth, divine order, and eternal law written upon the heart.

The Christ Within

Christ Consciousness is the awakened state of divine alignment, love, authority, and spiritual inheritance. It is the realization of Sonship with God — expressed through righteous living, wisdom, and peace.

“The Kingdom of God is within you.” — Luke 17:21
