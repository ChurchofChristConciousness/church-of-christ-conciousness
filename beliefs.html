The Christ Consciousness Church affirms belief in the divine presence of God, expressed through Christ Consciousness — a lived awareness of truth, compassion, stewardship, and sacred responsibility.

Worship is conducted through prayer, study, contemplation, and acts of service. Instruction is offered for spiritual development, moral formation, and the cultivation of wisdom in accordance with divine law.

Practices are non-commercial, non-political, and undertaken solely for the spiritual benefit of the congregation and the public.
